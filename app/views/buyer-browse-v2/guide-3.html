Browse Solutions - Supplier defined capabilities

Supplier defined capabilities are a different kind of capability

Instead of being pre-defined as with the other Capabilities, Suppliers have written their own description of the function they offer and defined their own Epics and Acceptance Criteria. These Supplier-defined Capabilities have been grouped into themes. You’ll be able to search using themes in the same way as regular Capabilities.

For example, several Suppliers may have defined their own capabilities, but those different Capabilities all meet a business need to send SMS texts. SMS will therefore be presented as a theme.
