Browse Solutions - Additional and associated services

A Catalogue Solution might have an optional 'Additional Service'

These are modules that provide additional Capabilities to a Catalogue Solution for an extra cost. When you browse and search Catalogue Solutions on the Buying Catalogue, if there are Additional Services available, information about the Capabilities and price will be presented to you.

There are also optional 'Associated Services'

These services help you to implement or optimise the live operation of a Catalogue Solution and/or Additional Services, for example, training.
